Fundamental Tenets

The core principles that guide our spiritual journey and fellowship

Prayer

Communication with God through prayer is the foundation of our spiritual life, fostering a deep and personal relationship with the Divine.

Worship

Worship is our expression of reverence and adoration for God, creating an atmosphere where His presence can dwell among us.

Word

The Holy Scriptures are our ultimate authority, providing guidance, wisdom, and revelation for righteous living.

Values and Virtues

The moral qualities that define our character as followers of Christ

Obedience

Wholehearted submission to God's commands and will as demonstrated through our actions and choices.

1 John 2:5-6, 1 John 5:3

Holiness

Living a life set apart for God, reflecting His purity and righteousness in all we do.

Hebrews 12:14, 1 Peter 1:15-16

Faith and Love

Unwavering trust in God's promises and selfless love for others as the greatest commandments.

Proverbs 3:3-4, Romans 12:10

Trustworthy

Being reliable and faithful in our commitments, reflecting God's faithfulness in our relationships.

Proverbs 3:5-6

Righteousness

Living in accordance with God's moral standards, pursuing justice and integrity in all aspects of life.

2 Timothy 2:19

Outreach Endeavors

An evangelical ministry that embodies the Great Commission, passionately pursuing souls for the Kingdom wherever they are.

Fellowship Structure

Our community is organized to foster spiritual growth and meaningful connections

Main Fellowship

  • Led by our Apostle
  • Primary teaching source
  • Spiritual covering for all groups
  • Monthly joint gatherings

Circle Representatives

  • Each circle leads a Bible study group
  • Responsible for regional coordination
  • Receive teachings from main fellowship
  • Facilitate monthly Kesha meetings

Regional Groups

  • Weekly Bible study meetings
  • Flexible scheduling based on members
  • Community outreach initiatives
  • Mutual support and accountability
